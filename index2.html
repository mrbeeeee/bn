About/Lunch

What is this?

This is bell.ninja! Yes, "ninja" is a TLD, or a top-level domain, similar to "com". Bell Ninja is a website that allows for easier access to information regarding Riviera Prepratory Schools' schedules and special events. Most visitors load the website to check when the next bell is going to ring. The website can very accurately tell when the next bell rings. (Well, most of the time) Unfortunately, the website's reliability for getting information about school events cannot be guaranteed because information is updated on the fly by a human. If you are looking for information regarding the school's COVID-19 response, be sure to go to their official website here: rivieraschools.com



If you seek more information about the website, feel free to contact me at support@bell.ninja
Thanks! This website is not owned or operated by Riviera Schools. It may be affiliated in that it is run and maintained by a student.

The count downtimer is off

In some cases, an incorrect entry can happen when a new schedule is inputted. If the schedule is ringing 5-15 minutes off, please alert me by emailing the email below (more contact information available in the contact section)

Bell Ninja calculates the difference between your time and the time of the next bell ring. In some rare cases, if your device time is wrong, the countdown timer will be wrong. Check if your tablet has the correct time by comparing it against an atomic clock. An excellent resource is a website called time.gov (Clickable)If your device's time is wrong, it might be a good idea to check with the administration to get your tablet sorted out. (All Riviera Schools tablets appear to have a restriction on general device settings, so fixing you're devices time cannot be done without the help of an administrator.)

Seeing "NaN aN aN aN"? You might be using an unsupported browser. The most compatible browser to use with Bell Ninja seems to be Google Chrome.

Mobile Device Compatability

Bell Ninja is now optimised for use with Windows tablets such as Microsoft's Surface Pro. You could use a smart phone to access the website, but be sure to maintain a level of tolerance. There may be visualization bugs and even functionality bugs.

Safari is the least compatible browser for Bell Ninja. IOS devices using Safari, such as iPhones, iPads, and Macs tend to not work with Bell Ninja.

Not Loading?

Check your wifi reception. If it is low, try getting indoors. Stay away from the field when trying to get the best wifi reception. Bell Ninja requires internet connection to refresh the time after the bell rings.

If the website still doesn't load, your browser may have some corrupt data that is preventing you from accessing certain websites. Try clearing your browsing cookies.
